Megenda

Make the most out of your meetings

Our Mission

We want your meetings to be effective. We are working hard to build an ecosystem that allows you to easily create, share, and use agendas in your meetings.

The Problem

Meetings get off topic, go long, or miss certain important topics. In short, they just aren't as effective as they could be.

Our Solution

Using Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) and Natural Language Understanding (NLU) we can help you talk about the important things. Megenda will gently help you stay on task, and remind you of important things left to discuss. Megenda will create a post meeting summary with a To Do list that can be sent to all who attended the meeting.
